# Scout Report — Iteration 104

## Gap: Empty spaces give no guidance — new users don't know what to do

A user creates their first space. Every lens is empty. The Board shows an empty kanban. The Feed is blank. No guidance, no suggested actions. For a platform where the core experience is "work alongside an AI agent," the first 30 seconds after space creation determine whether someone stays or leaves.

## What "Filled" Looks Like

When a space has zero nodes, each lens shows a contextual empty state with specific suggested actions: "Create a task and assign it to the Mind", "Start a conversation", "Write your first post." Each suggestion links directly to the action. The Board empty state is the most critical — it's the default view.
